- ✅ `<meta property="og:image:width" content="1200">`
- ✅ `<meta property="og:image:height" content="630">`

#### Twitter Cards
- ✅ Changed from `summary` to `summary_large_image`
- ✅ `<meta name="twitter:image" content="https://public.monster/social-card.png">`
