# Bunny Fonts Selection

## Findings

- **Comic Sans alternative:** Comic Neue (6 styles available)
- **Impact alternative:** Not available on Bunny Fonts

## Solution

Since Impact is not available on Bunny Fonts, we'll:
1. Use **Anton** or **Alfa Slab One** as Impact alternatives (bold display fonts)
2. Use **Comic Neue** as Comic Sans MS replacement
3. Keep system font fallbacks

## Font Stack
- Title (Impact replacement): Anton or Alfa Slab One
- Body (Comic Sans replacement): Comic Neue
